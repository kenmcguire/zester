About Me

My Journey Into Art

  • Street Art

    What I like

    Street Art is a fun way to display a message to the world. You can transform a plain and boring grey wall into something colourful and fun.

  • Letters

    My Start

    I started with letters after seeing a friend do it in 2016. It was challenging to get the letter stucture right to make it look even and adding style and flow to the letters was difficult.

  • Next Step

    Where I will go from here

    Next I am going to focus on imagry and political messages in my art.
